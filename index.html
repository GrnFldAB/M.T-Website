Structured data. Every transaction.

Each LYTEN™ material delivery is accompanied by a batch-specific data record aligned to EPCIS 2.0 and GS1 Digital Link standards. Fields are structured for direct import into CSRD reporting systems, DPP interfaces, and brand-level environmental databases. No manual data entry required on the buyer side.

The record captures provenance, fibre specification, processing methodology, certification status, and chain-of-custody events from farm gate to finished material handoff. Data is primary — generated at source, not modelled or averaged. The structure follows open standards to ensure compatibility with third-party DPP systems and future regulatory portals.
