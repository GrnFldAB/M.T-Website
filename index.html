The Wool-Blend Compromise.

The technical mid-layer category's answer to natural performance has been to blend it. Compromise it. Merino and polyester. Merino and Tencel. Merino with a synthetic resin treatment. Recycled polyester is still polyester: it sheds the same synthetic microfibres in the wash. Each iteration addresses the input. None resolves what happens next.

Microplastic Pathway

A polyester-face wool blend sheds synthetic microfibres every wash cycle. Recycling the input fibre does not change the shedding behaviour.

Blend Complexity

A bi-component wool-polyester fabric cannot be recycled as a single clean stream. Fibre separation at scale remains an unresolved infrastructure problem.

Chemistry Burden

Chlorine-Hercosett superwash processing generates AOX-contaminated wastewater. As regulatory scrutiny of "natural" claims tightens, that burden carries forward.

Circular by Design. Not by Disassembly.

A mono-material keratin structure requires no separation. It enters fibre-to-fibre recycling as a single clean stream. At the end of its useful life, it returns to the biosphere without leaving synthetic residue behind.

Structured data. Every transaction.

Each LYTEN™ material delivery is accompanied by a batch-specific data record aligned to EPCIS 2.0 and GS1 Digital Link standards. Fields are structured for direct import into CSRD reporting systems, DPP interfaces, and brand-level environmental databases. No manual data entry required on the buyer side.

The record captures provenance, fibre specification, processing methodology, certification status, and chain-of-custody events from farm gate to finished material handoff. Data is primary -- generated at source, not modelled or averaged. The structure follows open standards to ensure compatibility with third-party DPP systems and future regulatory portals.

Execution Model.

// 01. INTEGRATION

Does this require new software?

No. MONO•TERRA™ is a fibre ingredient brand, not a SaaS platform. Data is delivered as a standardised file (JSON/EPCIS) linked to the commercial invoice, designed for immediate ingestion into your existing PLM or traceability ecosystem.

// 02. VERIFICATION

How is data accuracy ensured?

We do not rely on generic regional averages. We deliver Primary Raw Data, measured to PEF standards and verified against farm records. Full transparency on methodology ensures a defensible audit trail.

// 03. CERTIFICATION

Does this replace RWS?

No, it enhances it. All fibre is RWS-certified for animal welfare. We layer granular primary data on top to deliver a specific cradle-to-gate footprint value for every batch.

// 04. SCALE

Is supply consistent?

Yes. We maintain strategic buffer stock to smooth seasonal fluctuations. For expansion, we leverage existing relationships with adjacent producers pre-assessed against the strict MONO•TERRA™ protocol.
